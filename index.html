The Word(le)

Guess the Bible-Themed Word in Six Tries!

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

- A Green tile means the letter is in the correct place.

- A Orange tile means the letter is in the word, but in the wrong place.

- A Grey tile means the letter is not in the word.

A new Bible Word will be available each day!
